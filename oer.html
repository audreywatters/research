OER in K-12


In 2012, I conducted research into the state of open educational reseources. My task wasn’t simply to identify where the repositories of OER exist or what they contain. Nor was I just looking at adoption or usage (although yes, the research did address all these things.) I was particularly keen to investigate the formats that these openly licensed materials are in, and by an extension whether they’re being remixed.

This work was sponsored by FunnyMonkey.
